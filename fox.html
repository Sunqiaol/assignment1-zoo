Fox

Foxes are small to medium-sized omnivorous mammals found in both rural and urban environments throughout the world. There are more than 30 species of foxes, but the most common and best known is the red fox. Foxes are known for their distinctive bushy tails and pointed ears. They are agile and skilled hunters, preying on a variety of small mammals, birds and insects. Foxes are also known for their intelligence and adaptability, able to thrive in a variety of environments.
